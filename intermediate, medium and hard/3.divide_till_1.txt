given n , how many times we need to divide n by 2 until it becomes 1?

- floor(n/2)

note : 
All the positive non-zero integers are called Natural numbers. All the positive integers, including zero, are called whole numbers.

sum of 1st n natural number - (n*(n+1))/2


sum of 1st whole number - ((n-1)*n)/2
